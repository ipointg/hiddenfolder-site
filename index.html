About

A solo enthusiast who builds things that seem interesting or useful — games, desktop apps, Telegram bots, visual novels, localizations. No strict genre, no strict format. If the idea catches me, I make it.

Hidden Folder Games is where all of that lives. Some projects take months, some years. All of them are made by one person, which means they’re slow but personal.
